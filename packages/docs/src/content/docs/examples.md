---
title: Examples
description: Questions you can ask your MCP client once connected to yawa.
---

You can ask questions about your traffic in plain language. Here are some examples to get you started.

## Traffic

- What are my top pages this week?
- How many visitors did I get yesterday?
- Show me pageviews by hour for today.

## Referrers and campaigns

- Which UTM campaigns are driving the most visitors?
- What are my top referrers this month?
- Where is my traffic coming from?

## Audience

- What browsers are my visitors using?
- Break down my visitors by device type.
- What languages do my visitors speak?

## Web Vitals

- Show me web vitals for my homepage.
- What's my LCP distribution this week?
- Which pages have the worst Core Web Vitals?

## Custom events

- What are my top custom events?
- How many signups did I get this week?
